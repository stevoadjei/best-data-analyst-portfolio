Project Overview

Stock Market Analysis

This project focuses on analyzing stock market data to uncover insights and trends. By examining historical stock prices and trading volumes, we aim to provide actionable insights for investors.

Objective: The primary goal is to analyze stock market data to identify trends and patterns. This analysis will enable investors to:

  • Make informed investment decisions.
  • Identify potential investment opportunities.

Approach:

  • Data Collection: Using Yahoo Finance API to fetch historical stock data.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis: Visualizing stock prices and trading volumes.
  • Insights & Visualization: Providing actionable insights based on the analysis.

Join us in exploring the stock market data and uncovering insights that can help investors make better decisions!

Dataset Overview

Shape: 1,000,000 rows and 6 columns.

  • Date (datetime): Date of the stock price.
  • Open (float): Opening price of the stock.
  • High (float): Highest price of the stock during the day.
  • Low (float): Lowest price of the stock during the day.
  • Close (float): Closing price of the stock.
  • Volume (int): Number of shares traded.

Missing Values: None detected in the dataset.

Duplicate: None detected in the dataset.
